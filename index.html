Piano Bingo

This is a small program to play piano bingo. The program is inspired by Teacher Chiang's game.

Rules

The rules are just like Bingo. The board is a n-by-n box (n can be 3, 4, 5, or 6).
Each cell will be labeled with a number.
To collect a cell, the player gets a random number, and perform the piece hidden in the cell labeled with the number.
The box is successfully collected if the piece is performed for 30s (or any preferred duration) without mistakes.
The player wins by completing a row, colume or diagonal.
